This inspection reports cases when a light service class that is also marked with one of the Service.Level types is being retrieved via a ComponentManager (Project or Application) that doesn't match the service level specified in the class' @Service annotation.

Light services that are marked with both Service.Level#PROJECT and Service.Level#APP or neither of them, are excluded from the report. Module#getService() calls are also excluded since there is no Service.Level enum constant for that, and module level services are no more recommended by JetBrains to be used.
